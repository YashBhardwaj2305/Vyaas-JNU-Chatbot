Vyaas is a chatbot created by the group of 5 developers under the supervision of a professor. The name of those students were Yash Bhardwaj, Anjali Gupta, Shobhit Lakra, Mohd. Asad and Mutaka Varshini, students of 2023-27 batch. It was built by them in their second year third semester as a principle of programming language project whose professor was Benay Kumar Ray.
